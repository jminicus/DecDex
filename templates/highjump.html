High Jump

The high jump is one of the most technical events in the decathlon. The aim is to jump and backbend over a bar, but there are many components of this event that have to work together smoothly for this to happen - the approach, takeoff, and arch. Below, store some cues to help you remember how to put all these moving parts together and jump high.
