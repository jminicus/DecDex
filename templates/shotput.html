Shotput

The shotput is a measure of pure power and strength - a decathlete has to launch a solid 16 pound shot as far as possible. Even though there is a lot of power involved, perfecting the shotput has a lot to do with grace, balance, and form. Below, store some cues to help you get in the proper positions to propel the shot far.
